Our Story
Started with a Single Wood-Fired Oven

The Ember House was born from a simple belief: great food doesn't need to be complicated — it just needs to be honest. We source locally, cook over real fire, and pour heart into every plate.

Fourteen years later, we're still cooking the same way. Come hungry. Leave happy.
