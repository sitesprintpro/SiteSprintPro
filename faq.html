Pricing & Payment
How much does a website cost?

We offer two packages. The Basic Build is $897 and covers up to 5 pages with everything a small business needs to be found online and generate calls. The Pro Build is $1,197 and includes up to 8 pages, a booking system, testimonials section, blog setup, advanced SEO, and priority delivery.

Both packages include 2 free maintenance service visits after launch. There are no hidden fees and no monthly platform charges from us.

Do I have to pay everything upfront?

No. We split it in half. You pay 50% to get started and the remaining 50% when we deliver the finished site for your review. You do not pay the second half until you have seen the site and approved it.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Zelle, Venmo, and Cash App. Payment details are included on every invoice. All payments go directly to SiteSprintPro — never to a sales representative.

Are there any monthly fees after my site launches?

No ongoing fees from us. Your only recurring cost is your domain name, purchased directly through Namecheap at roughly $12 per year — paid directly to them, not us.

We do offer optional maintenance plans starting at $100 per quarter if you want us to handle ongoing updates for you, but that is entirely your choice.

The Process
How long does it actually take to build my site?

Basic builds deliver in 2–3 business days. Pro builds deliver in 1–2 business days due to priority queue access. Once you fill out our short intake form and the deposit clears, we get to work immediately.

How much do I have to do? Do I need to write anything?

Very little. You fill out a short intake form — 7 questions about your business — and that is it. We write all the content for you: service descriptions, about section, calls to action, headlines, everything. You review the finished site and tell us if anything needs adjusting.

Can I make changes after I see the draft?

Yes. Basic builds include 2 revision rounds and Pro builds include 4 revision rounds. We send you a preview link first. You review it, send us your notes, and we update it before final delivery. Additional revisions after the included rounds are available at $75 per hour.

What do I need to provide?

Just your intake form answers. If you have photos of your business or team you would like to use, you can share them — but it is not required. If you do not have photos, we source professional imagery that fits your industry at no extra charge.

If you have an existing logo, great. If not, Pro builds include a custom text logo design.

Domain & Hosting
Do I need to buy a domain name?

Yes — and that is by design. You buy your own domain so you own it 100%. We walk you through purchasing it on Namecheap (typically around $12/year). We then connect it to your site for free. This means your website and domain always belong to you, not us.

Who hosts the website?

Your site is hosted on Netlify, one of the most reliable and fast hosting platforms available. It is free to host static sites there, which keeps your costs at zero beyond your domain renewal each year.

How long until my site is live after I approve it?

Once you approve the final version and your domain is pointed to your site, allow 24–48 hours for the domain to fully propagate across the internet. This is a standard DNS process — not a delay on our end. Your site is technically live immediately, but it takes that window for the address to work everywhere globally.

Quality & Results
Will my site show up on Google?

Every site we build includes basic on-page SEO from day one — proper title tags, meta descriptions, header structure, and fast load times. Pro builds include advanced SEO with full meta optimization. This gives you a strong foundation for Google to index your site. Ranking takes time and ongoing effort, but we make sure your site is set up correctly from the start.

Will it look good on a phone?

Every site we build is mobile-first. We design and test for phones before desktops because over 60% of your customers are searching from their phone. Click-to-call buttons, maps, and contact forms are all optimized for mobile use.

I already have a website. Is it worth replacing?

It depends on what you have. If your current site loads slowly, breaks on mobile, has no clear call-to-action, or looks outdated — it may actually be costing you customers rather than bringing them in.

We also offer a site refresh service starting at $300 if you prefer to rebuild without starting from scratch. Call us and we will take a look at what you currently have and give you an honest assessment.

Can I update the site myself after it is live?

Our sites are hand-coded for speed and quality, so they do not use a drag-and-drop editor. For ongoing changes, you can use one of our maintenance plans — we handle any update you need. Most clients find this easier than managing a site themselves, and it keeps everything looking professional.

Trust & Security
What if I am not happy with what you build?

You do not pay the second half until you have seen the site. If the draft does not meet your expectations, you use your revision rounds to get it right. We do not ask for the final payment until you are satisfied with the result.

Who do I pay — the person who called me or SiteSprintPro?

Always pay SiteSprintPro directly — never to a sales representative. Every invoice comes from us with our company information. Payment goes to our verified company accounts only. If anyone asks you to pay them personally, that is not our policy and you should contact us at 346-795-0020 immediately.

What happens if I do not respond or go quiet after the deposit?

We will follow up with you. If we cannot reach you within 5 business days after sending a preview or request for information, your project is placed on hold until we reconnect. Your deposit is held — not forfeited. We work with your schedule.
